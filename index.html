The McLean Club

McLean, VA

Our History

Founded in McLean, Virginia, The McLean Club is an invitation-oriented social and philanthropic club.

Membership is intentionally limited and is offered by invitation only.

The Club brings together neighbors and friends who value meaningful gatherings, civic service, and charitable work in the McLean community.

Our Mission

Central to our mission, The McLean Club supports local causes while fostering friendship, service, and a shared commitment to the community.

Through social events and philanthropic efforts, the Club creates opportunities for members to connect and contribute with purpose.
