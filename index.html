About Us

The McLean Club
McLean, Virginia

Founded in McLean, Virginia in 1998, The McLean Club is an invitation-oriented social and philanthropic club dedicated to friendship, service, and community engagement.

Membership is intentionally limited to 35 members and is offered by invitation only. The Club brings together neighbors and friends who value meaningful gatherings, civic involvement, and charitable work in the McLean community.

Our Mission

The McLean Club supports local causes while fostering friendship, service, and a shared commitment to the community.

Through social events and philanthropic efforts, the Club creates opportunities for members to connect, serve, and contribute with purpose.
